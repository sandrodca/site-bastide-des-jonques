Welcome

Enjoy your stay in a charming gîte, within the Luberon Park, in the heart of Provence

La Bastide des Jonquets offers you a comfortable, independent and authentic 40m² gîte, available for two adults : enjoy some relaxing holidays and a wellness ambiance in an ideal spot located 5 min from Apt.
