Lacoste

Medieval village and Marquis de Sade's castle

Village

Lacoste, between history and creation

Dominated by the imposing ruins of the Marquis de Sade's castle, Lacoste is a strikingly beautiful medieval village. Its cobbled streets, stone houses, and vaulted passages climb the hillside up to the castle ruins, offering magnificent views over the Luberon.

Practical info

Plan your visit

Distance

12km — about 15 minutes by car.

Duration

2 to 3 hours for the village and castle.

Not to miss

The castle, medieval streets, Lacoste Festival in summer.
