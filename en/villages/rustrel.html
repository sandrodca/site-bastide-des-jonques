Village

The Colorado Provençal of Rustrel

A former ochre mining site, the Colorado Provençal of Rustrel is an exceptional natural site that offers a striking spectacle of flamboyant colors. Its ochre cliffs, fairy chimneys, and winding paths transport you to an almost unreal landscape, with hues ranging from golden yellow to intense red.

Practical info

Plan your visit

Distance

3km — accessible on foot, by bike, or 5 minutes by car.

Duration

Allow 2 to 3 hours to explore the main trails.

Good to know

Bring good walking shoes. The site has an entrance fee.
