City

Avignon, the City of Popes

Capital of Christianity in the 14th century, Avignon is an exceptional city listed as a UNESCO World Heritage site. Its imposing Palais des Papes, the largest Gothic palace in Europe, dominates the city and bears witness to its prestigious past.

Practical info

Plan your visit

Distance

50km — about 50 minutes by car. TGV train station.

Duration

Full day minimum to enjoy the city.

Not to miss

Palais des Papes, Pont d'Avignon, Festival in July.
