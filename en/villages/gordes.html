Gordes

Iconic perched village of the Luberon

Village

Gordes, the sentinel of Luberon

Ranked among the Most Beautiful Villages of France, Gordes is undoubtedly the most photographed village in the Luberon. Perched on an imposing limestone rock, its dry-stone houses spiral around its Renaissance castle, offering a spectacular silhouette visible from afar.

The village itself is a labyrinth of cobbled streets, vaulted passages, and small shaded squares. The castle, which now houses the Pol Mara Museum, can be visited and offers a breathtaking view over the Calavon valley.

A few kilometers from Gordes, don't miss the Sénanque Abbey, a jewel of Cistercian Romanesque art nestled in a valley surrounded by lavender fields. A place of absolute peace and beauty.
